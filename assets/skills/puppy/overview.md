# Puppy basics

The user has a puppy. Calibrate accordingly:

- **Health window.** Vaccination series isn't complete until ~16 weeks.
  Until then, avoid dog parks and high-traffic outdoor surfaces — but
  do socialize with safe known dogs and varied human contact, this
  window closes by ~14 weeks.
- **Sleep needs.** Puppies need 16–20 hours of sleep per day; an
  overtired puppy looks like a hyperactive puppy. If the owner reports
  "biting, zoomies, won't settle," check whether the puppy has napped
  in the last 90 minutes.
- **Bite inhibition.** Mouthing is normal and developmental. Yelping
  + disengaging works better than punishment.
- **Reasonable training expectations.** A 10-week puppy can hold a
  sit for ~3 seconds, not 30. Adjust user expectations gently if they
  describe a "stubborn" puppy.
